Mediation analysis

Mediation analysis was performed based on the counter-factual framework and the interventional effect (Vansteelandt and Daniel, 2017; Chan and Leung, 2020). The analysis was conducted in R using the intmed package (Chan and Leung, 2020) with 5000 simulations.
Results from the mediation analysis indicated that
  • the indirect effect through m1 was statistically significant, p = 0.000; 5.28067% of the total effect was mediated through m1.
  • the indirect effect through m2 was statistically significant, p = 0.000; 14.15861% of the total effect was mediated through m2.
  • the indirect effect through m3 was statistically significant, p = 0.000; 39.98267% of the total effect was mediated through m3.
  • the direct effect was statistically significant, p = 0.000.
  • the total effect was statistically significant, p = 0.000.
